About Us!

Fish-R-Us is a nonprofit organization dedicated to informing the masses on the wonderfully interesting ocean world! We provide a webite that contains hundreds of links to interesting and scholarly articles about your favorite marine species, perfect for citing in your papers. We also provide the best, most up-to-date information on hot fishing spots and seasonal fish migration patterns. You can also sign up for a free account to gain exclusive access to new articles as soon as they are released, weekly email updates on the Fish-R-Us team, and special opportunities to win free stuff! It is said that only 5% of the worlds oceans have been thoroughly explored and studied. Join us and learn about the other 95% as soon as we do!
